Instructions

This is a one-player game against the computer, that follows the classic rules of "Rock-Paper-Scissors". Each round, the player and computer select one of the three hands. If there is a tie, there is no round winner, and the round is repeated. If not a tie, then there is a round winner:

Rock beats Scissors,

Scissors beats Paper, and

Paper beats Rock

To begin, adjsut the number of rounds you would like to play (if needed), and then click "Start". The winner of the match is he who wins the majority of the rounds (eg: 3 out of 5 rounds).

Clicking on "Cancel" during a match will end the match, but will leave the current game statistics visible. Clicking on "Start" during a match will clear all current game statistics and begin a new game.

At any time, you can click on "Instructions" to get help. Click again on the "Instructions" button to hide the panel.

Enjoy Playing!
